History

Charter Year: 1850

Renowned for: Brigham Young University (BYU), outdoor recreation, and technology startups

Significant Events:

  • Founded by Mormon pioneers and named after French-Canadian trapper Étienne Provost
  • Home to Brigham Young University, established in 1875, one of the largest private religious universities in the U.S.
  • Became a major tech hub in the 21st century, nicknamed part of "Silicon Slopes"
