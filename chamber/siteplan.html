Project: Provo Chambers of Commerce

Site Plan

Site Purpose

The Provo Chamber of Commerce exists to support and promote local businesses, encourage networking opportunities, and enhance economic growth in Provo, Utah. Our website serves as a central hub for business owners, students, and residents to stay informed about events, resources, and opportunities available in the community.

Target Market

We serve business owners, entrepreneurs, students at Brigham Young University (BYU), and residents of Provo, Utah. The city has a strong presence of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, and many businesses and community initiatives align with family and faith-based values.

SEO Plan

  • Use localized keywords such as "Provo business networking" and "Provo small business resources".
  • Encourage member businesses to link back to the chamber website to boost domain authority.
  • Regularly publish blog posts about local business success stories and upcoming events.
  • Ensure all images have alt-text descriptions with relevant keywords.
  • Embed Google Analytics and Google Business Profile integration for better local search visibility.
